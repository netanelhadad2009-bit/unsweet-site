Unsweet — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Unsweet mobile application ("Unsweet", "the App").

1. Introduction

Unsweet ("we", "our", or "the App") is operated by Netanel Hadad. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Unsweet mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Health & nutrition data: Meal logs, sugar intake, streak history, weight logs, mood logs, and craving logs you voluntarily enter.
  • Photos: Food images you upload for analysis (stored securely, never shared).
  • Device information: Device type, OS version, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility.
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to improve the app experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide food analysis, sugar detection, and personalized tracking features.
  • To maintain your streak history and progress data.
  • To improve the app based on anonymous usage patterns.
  • To send important service-related notifications.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled on all tables. Food images are stored in secure cloud storage. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting.
  • OpenAI: Food image analysis (images are processed and not stored by OpenAI).
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management.
  • PostHog, Mixpanel, AppsFlyer: Anonymous analytics.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the service providers listed above to operate the app.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Export your data.
  • Opt out of analytics tracking.

8. Children's Privacy

Unsweet is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@unsweetapp.com
